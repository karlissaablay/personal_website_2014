    Why, hello!

    Software Developer by day and Graphic Designer by night, I'm a web-designing, 2D/3D-animating, tea-loving artiste. Whether it's debugging C++ or painstakingly modeling a new character, I spend countless hours typing away on the keyboard.

    I graduated from the University of Colorado at Boulder with a degree in Computer Science and a minor in Technology, Arts, and Media in May 2014. Currently residing in Boulder, CO, and enjoying 300 days of sunshine, I'm searching for an amazing team with whom I can grow both my development and artistic abilities.

    So in short, I adore making everything look pretty and work well through technology - be it through UI Development, UX Design or Sofware Development.
